Hot Chocolate

Description

Learn how to make hot chocolate from scratch on your stovetop.

Ingredients

  • Milk or cream (Animal or plant-based)
  • White sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • Mini marshmallows
  • Chocolate shavings

Steps

  1. Pour water into a small saucepan, bring to a boil and whisk in cocoa powder and sugar.
  2. Lower heat and whisk in the milk. Cook until milk is heated but not boiling.
  3. Mix in chocolate chips, and whisk until melted.
  4. Remove mixture from stovetop and stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Transfer mixture to a cup, top with marshmallows and chocolate shavings.
