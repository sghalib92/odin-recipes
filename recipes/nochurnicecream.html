No Churn Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

Make this cool, delicious no churn ice cream at home with only four igredients. No ice cream maker needed!

Ingredients

  • Sweetened condensed milk (or sweetened condensed coconut milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Sea salt

Steps

  1. In a large, chilled bowl, whisk together the sweetened condensed milk, vanilla extract and salt.
  2. In a second large, chilled bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  3. Fold half of the cooled whipped cream into the milk mixture.
  4. Pour the combined mixture into the remaining whipped cream, folding gently to further combine. Mix any ingredients you would like (chocolate chips, nuts, etc).
  5. Pour mixture into a chilled 9" x 5" loaf pan, lined with parchment or wax paper.
  6. Chill in freezer for at least four hours before serving.
