Overnight Oats

Description

This overnight oats recipe is a healthy, quick and simple breakfast that you can make ahead for busy mornings and customize with as many add-ins and toppings as you like!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Milk or plant-based milk alternative
  • Chia seeds (optional)
  • Greek or vegan yogurt (optional)
  • Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Sweetner of your choice
  • Toppings (fresh or dried fruit, nuts, seeds, cinnamon and other spices)

Steps

  1. To start, combine oats and chia seeds in a bowl, mason jar, or any sealable container of your choice.
  2. Add Greek (or vegan yogurt) and vanilla extract.
  3. Add sweetner of choice (brown sugar, honey, maple syrup).
  4. Add milk (or vegan alternative) and mix until you see no clumps.
  5. Seal or cover jar with a lid, and chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  6. Next morning, add any fruit toppings, nuts or seeds of your choice and dig in!
